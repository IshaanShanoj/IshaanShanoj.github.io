Fairview International School Penang

Fairview International School Penang is an IB school that is located in Penang, Malaysia. They are one of the schools that follows the popular International Baccalaureate (IB) program. They were established in late 2006.

There are other Fairview International School campuses in KL, Ipoh, etc. There are a total of 5 campuses in Malaysia, which are located in KL, SJ, Ipoh, JB, and Penang.
